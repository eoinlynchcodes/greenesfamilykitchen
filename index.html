Our Story


Greenes family Kitchen located at our family farm in Rockfield , Rathaspick, Rathowen, Co. Wesmeath. Our family have been involved in faming and caring for the land for Generations.. We are passionate about sustainability, and pride ourselves in producing the finest quality foods.

We are consumer focused developing innovative foods from award winning free range eggs from our own farm, we have also linked up with other Irish producers while using their ingredients in our products. We place quality and taste at the core of what we do.
